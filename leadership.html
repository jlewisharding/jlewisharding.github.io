Leadership Updates

English

There are more big changes to announce.

Most of you know that several months ago I stepped down as R5 to focus on getting my personal life back in balance. Shorty stepped in and has done a fantastic job of keeping this alliance from dissolving. And we finished higher in the Season rankings than we ever have and with way fewer people active than we’ve had in the past. Many of you have no idea the amount of work and negotiating it takes to get through a season because most of the communication happens among R5s in discord channels. No one has the time to manage all of that and then communicate every single thing that comes up along the way. It takes a lot of trust in knowing that sometimes situations that you think are bad are actually “the best terrible option we could arrange.” So again, I want to thank Shorty for everything she did, especially when some of our long-standing, strong members were leaving and many of the rest of us were planning to do so. She refused to let us all fall apart, and I’m very grateful for that.

All of that being said, things in my life have achieved a bit more harmony and so Shorty and I are evolving the leadership of the alliance. I am going to take over R5 tonight and handle this transition to the new alliance. Shorty will rotate back in as R5 most likely the next time the server president swaps back to Pickle. We will likely continue swapping at these same times, or we may arrange a situation where Short is R5 during the seasons and I am R5 during the off-season.

Shorty isn’t going anywhere when I take over. This has been and continues to be a collaborative effort. Shorty and I communicate a lot about this game and have been even when I stepped away. What’s more, we have a tremendous group of R4s who always provide feedback and advice about how things are going.

Speaking of R4s, you are going to notice some changes there, too. HMB is going to have 3 seats when they get here. A fourth seat will be opened and left that way for a few weeks while we see if there are any other transfers who are interested and might be able to bring knowledge and perspective from their former servers. You can expect some rotations of familiar faces in the coming months. It might take some getting used to, but learn fast because we are going to be a completely different alliance soon.

Again, I want to thank you all for your patience. I know this seems silly because we are playing a game, but I believe at this point we know that most of us see this as more than just a way to pass time while we are on the toilet. If you have any questions or concerns about this, just think about what I always tell Don. Just kidding. Ask me or Shorty whatever you want.
